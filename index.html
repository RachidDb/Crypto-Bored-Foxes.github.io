Crypto Bored Foxes

Welcome to The Crypto Bored Foxes

The Crypto Bored Foxes Collection is a fixed set of 449 NFTs, each one of these items is stored on the ethereum blockchain

The idea of the Collection was inspired by the red nevada Foxes wich are endangered by climate change, which has already caused hotter and drier conditions in the Sierra Nevada and is projected to shrink the fox's habitat as temperatures warm and push the animal farther up mountain slopes.
