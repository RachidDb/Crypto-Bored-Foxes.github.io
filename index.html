Welcome to The Crypto Bored Foxes

Crypto Bored Foxes is an NFT collection consisting of 449 unique digital collectibles featuring different fox characters depicted in various mundane and uneventful scenarios. Each NFT in the collection is a one-of-a-kind artwork that has been minted on the Ethereum blockchain, making it a unique and scarce digital asset that can be bought, sold, and traded like any other cryptocurrency. The fox characters in the collection are depicted in a wide range of relatable, everyday scenarios, such as smoking, feeling bored, being in love, having weird looking outfits , or just hanging out. These scenarios are meant to evoke a sense of familiarity and comfort, as the viewer is invited to imagine themselves in the same position as the bored foxes.
